Workshop 1 Artifact

AI Lab: Building and Exploring an AI Chatbot

During the AI Lab, I explored how conversational Artificial Intelligence systems process natural language and generate human-like responses. This activity introduced me to the practical applications of Large Language Models (LLMs) and demonstrated how generative AI can assist users by answering questions, generating content, solving problems, and supporting decision-making.

Artifact Overview

The AI Lab focused on interacting with an AI chatbot using carefully designed prompts to observe its reasoning, response quality, and adaptability across different types of questions. Throughout the lab, I experimented with prompt engineering techniques, evaluated chatbot responses, and reflected on both the strengths and limitations of conversational AI.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Prompt Engineering
  • Critical Thinking
  • Problem Solving
  • Technical Communication
  • Evaluation of AI-generated Responses

Learning Outcomes

This experience improved my understanding of how AI chatbots interpret natural language and generate context-aware responses. I learned that the quality of chatbot outputs depends heavily on the clarity and specificity of user prompts. The lab also highlighted the importance of verifying AI-generated information and recognizing that generative AI should complement, rather than replace, human judgment.

Reflection

Completing this AI Lab strengthened my interest in Artificial Intelligence and reinforced the importance of prompt engineering when interacting with modern AI systems. I gained valuable experience in evaluating chatbot responses, understanding the capabilities of generative AI, and recognizing its current limitations. As I continue my graduate studies in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, this experience serves as an important foundation for future work involving machine learning, natural language processing, and intelligent systems.

Artifact 2 — AI-Generated Landing Page (Single-Prompt Activity)

Sweet Bloom Café Landing Page

For this in-class activity, I generated a complete, production-style landing page from a single descriptive prompt. The goal was to see how far a well-structured prompt alone could take a web project: one instruction produced a fully responsive, single-file site with its own styling, layout, and interactivity — no manual coding required.

The result is the Sweet Bloom Café landing page: a boutique dessert-and-coffee storefront featuring a hero, menu, gallery, reviews, and a contact section. It demonstrates how generative AI can turn a single natural-language request into a usable, visually polished web artifact, and reinforced the prompt-engineering lessons from the AI Lab.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Prompt Engineering (single-shot generation)
  • Generative AI for Web Design
  • Evaluating AI-Generated Output
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Design Communication

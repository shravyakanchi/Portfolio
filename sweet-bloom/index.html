Our story

Started with one stubborn sourdough starter

Maya opened Sweet Bloom with a rented oven, a second-hand espresso machine, and a starter named Doug that refused to die. Ten years later Doug is still here, still bubbling, still making the best croissant on Blossom Street.

Nothing arrives frozen. Everything is finished by hand, in the morning, by people who taste it before you do.

  • Butter, eggs and berries from farms inside the Willamette Valley
  • Beans roasted every Tuesday by our friends four blocks east
  • Cakes baked at dawn, never the night before
  • Anything unsold at close goes to the shelter on 9th
